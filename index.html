At Momo yogurt cafe, we aim to create a comfortable environment for members of the community to work, study, and hang out.

“Momo” means peach in japanese.

We make our yogurt from ethically sourced, organic ingredients and serve specialy and seasonal yogurt flavors.Our cafe is located in San Francisco, CA and has a large space for seating as well as plenty of tables and electrical outlets. We provide individual and group seating to accommodate different working styles.
